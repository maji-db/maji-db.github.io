Title Screen


ID Link


Links your game account with a klabID, a facebook account or a google play account (for android users). This is also the button for Data Transfer. You can read more about how to back up your account and transfer it to another device on this guide.

Data Restore


It's a troubleshooting function you use when you have problems playing the game. This function deletes all game data and redownloads it all over again, saving you the trouble of deleting and redownloading the game yourself. It's uncertain if your player data will remain untouched.

Bundle Download


This function is for people that doesn't like to wait for every single image in the game to appear. The bundle download stores all the data inside your device, making the game much bigger - but also much faster to load. The download amount is around 1GB.

Contact Us


The contact form is on this button.

Home Screen


Notices (お知らせ)


Shows all announcements ingame: upcoming events, gachas, bugs & updates.


Present (プレゼント)


Your gift box, where most ingame rewards will go; being them login bonuses, Four-Dimensional Mansion rewards or gifts from Klab.


Mission (ミッション)


Where you can see and claim the rewards of your Daily, Events & Total Missions. Click Missions for more information.

Total Strength (全総合力)


The Total Strength is the sum of Power of all the characters you possess. The Total Strength Level can be increased by tapping the Total Strength Button after it reaches the required value. When you increase the Total Strength, you get the Total Strength Bonus according to its level. The Total Strength Bonus is automatically added to the status of all characters you possess. You can check the character's statuses before and after the bonus by tapping the Total Strength Bonus Button at the Character's Detail Screen.



Menu


Friend (フレンド)


Shows all the friends you're following and the ones that are following you.


Player Info (プレイヤー情報)


Shows your player info like name, comment, player ID and current rank position on pvp. You can also change your Friend Character on this page.


Help (ヘルプ)


This button explains how everything works on this game (the text is in japanese, of course).

Agreement Rules (各種規約)


The agreement rules show the player's responsibilities and policies when playing the game. There are 5 buttons: x, Copyright, x, x and Privacy Policy.
