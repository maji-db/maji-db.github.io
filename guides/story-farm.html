Story Quest Farming

In the Story Quests we can get a lot of limit-breaking materials depending on the stage and arc they belong to. If you're short on some of them and doesn't have anymore crystal keys, the best option would be to farm a story stage that drops the material you want.

The materials change depending on the difficulty of the stage, as shown below:

Easy Difficulty Medium Difficulty Hard Difficulty
Spirit Crystal materials only Star Spirit materials only both materials


Spirit Crystals are used to limit-break Main Units, while Star Spirit are used on Support Units.

Every Story Arc drops a different type of material, and every stage has its own available attributes they can drop. To see which stage drops the right attribute of the material you want, click the arc image and a stage list will drop down.
