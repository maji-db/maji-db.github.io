Start Dash Gacha


The Start Dash Gacha is the first Gacha you get to play in the game, during the tutorial. In there, you can get your first 5★ Main Character Unit out of a fixed list of characters. The units you might be able to obtain are:


The best unit you can get from this gacha is either Kurama, Yusuke or Hiei. Kurama is an excellent DEF type that can recover his HP once when his health is low, while Yusuke has an AOE special and Hiei's basic attacks are one of the strongest in the game so far. If you didn't get the unit you wanted in your first pull, you can play the gacha over and over until you get the character you were looking for by pressing the button highlighted in the image below.
It might take a few tries for said character to appear, but keep pulling and he's certain to show up.

Premium Gacha


The Premium Gacha is the default gacha of the game. It's always going to be there, regardless of the current events & campaigns running at the time. The Premium Gacha contains all the common units in the game and grants you 1 5★ Certain Mini Gacha Ticket per single pull, and 10 tickets per multi pull. Once you collect 50 Tickets you can pull in the 5★ Certain Mini Ticket Gacha for a guaranteed 5★ unit. The Single Pull costs 25 Spirit Stones and the Multi Pull costs 250 Spirit Stones.

Maji Pick Up Gacha


The Maji Pick Up Gacha chooses 3 units (2 Main Characters and 1 Support Character), making them guaranteed units whenever you pull a 5★. This gacha is a good opportunity for people who are looking for specific characters since you'll always have 1/3 chance of getting the unit you want whenever you pull a 5★. The rates are the same as the Premium Gacha (3%), though, so it's still hard to pull one. The Maji PIick Up Gacha also grants 1 5★ Certain Mini Gacha Tickets per pull and guarantees you'll get one of the pick up units if you make 5 multi pulls.
