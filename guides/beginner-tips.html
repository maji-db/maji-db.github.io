About the Game

Yu Yu Hakusho 100 Maji Battle is a gacha game where you can play as Yu Yu Hakusho characters to beat quests & events. Most characters are obtained through gacha, being them free, paid, or with the game's currency: Spirit Stones. After you got some characters you're ready to begin the game.

Where to Start


You should start by playing the story quests. It's important to beat the story quests in all difficulties because they unlock character quests that gives you Soul Pieces to strenghthen your characters. (You can read more about it on Character Strengthening).

About Events


Can new players participate in events? Short answer is yes. The long answer is depends. Events have a lot of stages in all difficulties so you can farm the easier stages, even if they drop a smaller amount of medals. So it is possible to play events and actually get the important rewards. You'll just take more time than others at first. To beat the last stage of an event you'll most likely need a full team of 5★s.

Making Units Stronger

Game Mechanics


In Maji Battle you can skip daily quests and repeat quests automatically after you've 3★ed them, so make good use of those features when you play. After you click on a quest you'll be prompted to select a friend to help you defeat it. You'll be able to use this character's special once, so pick the strongest unit you can find.


Resume

■ Clear all daily missions every day.
■ Play the Free Gacha twice a day.
■ Clear the Story Quests in all difficulties to unlock Character Quests.
■ Sell 3★ and under rarities Soul Pieces to exchange for items in the exchange shop. Save 4★ Soul Pieces to increase your Overall Power.
■ Get the free farmable unit to rank SSS in events.
■ Beat the tower to get a free SSS 4★ unit.
■ If you want a specific unit, wait for it to show up in a Maji Pick Up Gacha for a better chance to get it.
